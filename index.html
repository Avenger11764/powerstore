Welcome to the Power Store Tournament! The goal is to strategically use Power Cards to outwit your opponents. You start with 5 starter Power Coins (PC). Use them wisely to buy cards from the store and gain an advantage.

To begin, use this command in the group chat:

/start

This will register you in the game and grant your starting coins.

üìú Gameplay Rules

Card Cooldown

There is a 15-minute cooldown between using cards. The ‚ö°Ô∏è Speed card can cut this in half, and the üîÄ Frenzy card lets you bypass it for two uses.

Card Uniqueness

You can only hold one of each type of card at a time. Use your card before buying another of the same kind!

Card Usage Locations

Cards are split into two types: Strategic Cards must be used in a private chat (DM) with the bot. Action Cards must be used in the official group chat. Using a card in the wrong location will not work.
