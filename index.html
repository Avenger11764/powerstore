Welcome to the Power Store Tournament! The goal is to strategically use Power Cards to outwit your opponents. You start with 50 Power Coins (PC). Use them wisely to buy cards from the store and gain an advantage.

To begin, use this command in the group chat:

/start

This will register you in the game and grant your starting coins.

üìú Gameplay Rules

Card Cooldown

There is a 15-minute cooldown between using cards. The ‚ö°Ô∏è Speed card can cut this in half, and the üîÄ Frenzy card lets you bypass it for two uses.

Card Uniqueness

You can only hold one of each type of card at a time. Use your card before buying another of the same kind!

Private Start & Purchases

To join the game and buy cards, you must interact with the bot in a private message (DM). Send /start and use /store in your DM to keep your inventory secret.

Public Card Use & Penalties

Power cards must only be used in the main group chat. Using a card in a private DM will result in a 100 Power Coin penalty from the admin.
